**Summer Sunset Pizza**

As the summer months approach in Vancouver, we're craving lighter, fresher flavors that won't weigh us down. That's why we've created a personalized pizza recipe just for you!

**Meet Your Summer Sunset Pizza:**

Crust: Made with cauliflower flour and chickpea protein, this crust is not only gluten-friendly but also packed with plant-based protein to support your sedentary job as a software engineer.

Sauce: Fresh tomatoes from local farmers provide the perfect balance of sweetness and acidity. This summer sauce is made with just a hint of olive oil and herbs for added flavor.

Veggies: Zucchini, bell peppers, and onions add natural sweetness and crunch to this pizza. These veggies are also rich in antioxidants and fiber to support your gut health goals.

Cheese: A blend of vegan mozzarella and cashew-based ricotta provides creamy texture without the dairy. This plant-based cheese alternative is perfect for a flexitarian like you!

Toppings: Fresh basil adds a pop of color and flavor, while a sprinkle of nutritional yeast enhances the cheesy goodness.

**Your Summer Sunset Pizza Ingredients:**

* 1 cup cauliflower flour (rich in fiber and antioxidants)
* 1/2 cup chickpea protein (plant-based protein for sustained energy)
* 1/4 cup olive oil (healthy fats for heart health)
* 2 cups fresh tomatoes (antioxidants and lycopene for immune support)
* 1 medium zucchini, sliced (cancer-fighting compounds and fiber)
* 1 bell pepper, sliced (vitamin C and antioxidants)
* 1 small onion, sliced (prebiotic fiber for gut health)
* 1/4 cup vegan mozzarella (plant-based protein and calcium for strong bones)
* 2 tbsp cashew ricotta (creamy texture without dairy)
* 1 tsp nutritional yeast (B vitamins and protein for energy)
* Fresh basil leaves (antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties)

This Summer Sunset Pizza is the perfect blend of flavors and nutrition to support your fitness goals. With a balance of plant-based protein, fiber, and healthy fats, you'll be energized and satisfied all summer long.

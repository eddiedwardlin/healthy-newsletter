Greek-style salad recipe

A simple and colourful salad packed full of Mediterranean flavour.
Prep: 15 mins
Serves 4

Per serving (1/4 recipe)
544kJ / 131kcal
6.6g protein
4.2g carbohydrate, of which 3.9g sugars
9.4g fat, of which 2.8g saturates
1.8g fibre
0.7g salt

Ingredients
juice of half lemon
2 tablespoons olive oil
0.5 teaspoon dried oregano
large pinch of black pepper
200g cherry tomatoes, halved
1 red onion finely sliced
half a cucumber cucumber, sliced and quartered
8 olives, quartered
100g reduced-fat Greek-style salad cheese (feta)

Method
Mix the lemon juice, olive oil, oregano and black pepper together.
Combine the salad vegetables, olives and dressing, mix well and put in a serving bowl.
Information:
You could add some diced pepper too.
Crumble the cheese over the top.
Information:
Serve with some crusty bread or wholemeal pittas to make a light meal.

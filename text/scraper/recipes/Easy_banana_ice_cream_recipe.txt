Easy banana ice cream recipe

Make this delicious 1-ingredient banana ice cream in 3 simple steps.
Prep: 5 mins
Serves 4

Per serving:
272kJ / 65kcal
1g protein
16g carbohydrate, of which 14g sugars
0.1g fat, with trace amount saturates
1.1g fibre
trace amount salt

Ingredients
4 very ripe bananas

Method

Freeze for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
Add to a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy. Scoop into bowls and tuck in!

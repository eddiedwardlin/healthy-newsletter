Tasty tuna and sweetcorn pasta recipe

On the table in 30 minutes, this is a quick and simple recipe that will boost your 5 A Day intake and also includes a portion of fish – a great source of protein.
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves 4

Per serving (1/4 recipe):
1,774kJ / 406kcals
31g protein
3.6g fat of which 0.75g saturates
64g carbohydrate of which 10g sugars
9g fibre
0.5g salt

Ingredients
300g wholemeal pasta shapes
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed or finely chopped
400g chopped tomatoes (1 tin, or fresh)
1 tablespoon tomato purée
150g sweetcorn (fresh, or tinned and drained)
2 teaspoons dried mixed herbs
2 tins tuna in water, drained and flaked

Method
Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet.
While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large non-stick frying pan. Add the onion and garlic and cook gently until soft, about 4-5 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, purée, sweetcorn and mixed herbs, and cook for another 5 minutes.
A ccouple of minutes before serving, add the tuna and stir through gently.
Information:
Fresh herbs add a delicious flavour, so try adding a few torn-up basil leaves or some chopped fresh parsley.
When the pasta is cooked, drain well and serve with the tuna and sweetcorn sauce.
Information:
This recipe can be served cold as a salad, making it perfect for packed lunches.

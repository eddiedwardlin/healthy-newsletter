Warm bananas and yoghurt recipe

A crafty way to get the kids to eat more fruit!
Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 3 mins
Serves 4

Per serving (with 1 tablespoon of low-fat yoghurt)
720kJ / 172kcal
4g protein
26g carbohydrate, of which 24g sugars
6g fat, of which 3g saturates
1g fibre
0.1g salt

Ingredients
20g low-fat spread
4 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced into diagonal chunks
zest and juice of 1 orange
4 tablespoons low-fat Greek-style natural yoghurt

Method
Heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium-low heat and add the low-fat spread.
Add the banana pieces and fry gently for 2 minutes. Add the orange juice and cook for another 30 to 40 seconds. Sprinkle with the grated orange zest.
Information:
Take care not to overcook the bananas, or else they will go very mushy.
Divide into bowls and top each portion with 1 tablespoon of low-fat Greek yoghurt.

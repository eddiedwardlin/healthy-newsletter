Banana and apricot bagels recipe

Such a simple idea â€“ yet these fruity bagels taste so good!
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 2 mins
Serves 4

Per serving:
1,020kJ / 244kcal
11.5g protein
46g carbohydrate, of which 18g sugars
2g fat, of which 1g saturates
5g fibre
0.7g salt

Ingredients
4 wholemeal bagels, sliced in half
80g low-fat soft cheese
40g ready-to-eat dried apricots, chopped
2 bananas, sliced

Method
Preheat the grill. Lightly toast the cut sides of each bagel.
Mix the low-fat soft cheese and apricots together and spread over the bagel bases. Top with sliced banana then sandwich the bagel tops in place.
Information:
If you cannot eat dairy, leave out the soft cheese. Mash the banana and spread over the toasted bagel, then sprinkle the chopped apricots on top.
